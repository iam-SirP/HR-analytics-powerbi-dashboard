# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing HR data to uncover workforce trends and provide actionable insights for HR and business leaders. The dashboard was developed in Power BI to help monitor employee demographics, retention, promotion readiness, job satisfaction, overtime patterns, and workforce distribution.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Cleaning and Transformation
* Data Visualization

## Key Insights

### Workforce Overview

* Total Employees: 1,470
* Male Employees: 882 (60%)
* Female Employees: 588 (40%)
* Active Employees: 1,353 (92%)
* Retired/Exited Employees: 117 (8%)

### Employee Demographics

* The largest employee groups fall within the 25–34 and 35–44 age ranges.
* The workforce is largely composed of employees in their prime working years, providing a strong foundation for productivity and organizational growth.

### Promotion Analysis

* 72 employees (4.9%) are currently due for promotion.
* The analysis highlights potential opportunities to strengthen career development and succession planning initiatives.

### Job Satisfaction

* Most employees reported medium to high job satisfaction.
* Approximately 20% reported low satisfaction, indicating an area that may require further investigation and engagement strategies.

### Overtime Analysis

* The majority of employees do not work overtime.
* This suggests a balanced workload and potentially healthier work-life integration across the organization.

## Business Value

This dashboard demonstrates how HR data can be transformed into meaningful insights that support workforce planning, employee engagement, talent management, and strategic decision-making.
